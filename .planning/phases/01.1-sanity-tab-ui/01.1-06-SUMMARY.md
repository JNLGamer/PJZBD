## Why It Got Built This Way

### Task 1 verification: vanilla resize and chrome behavior

Before applying Tasks 2-3, ran a static read on vanilla source to confirm planning assumptions.

**Claim 1: ISTabPanel does NOT propagate resize to child views — CONFIRMED.**
- Verified at ProjectZomboid/media/lua/client/ISUI/ISTabPanel.lua:457-479 (addView): only `view:setY(self.tabHeight)` and `addChild(view)` are called — no setWidth, no setHeight.
- No setWidth/setHeight/onResize methods that resize self.viewList items.

**Claim 2: ISCharacterScreen render-time setWidthAndParentWidth walks UP only — CONFIRMED.**
- Verified at ProjectZomboid/media/lua/client/ISUI/ISUIElement.lua:180-189: loop walks `parent = parent.parent` upward, never iterates child arrays.
- Mirror at lines 191-199 for setHeightAndParentHeight.

**Claim 3: All 5 vanilla tab views call `o:noBackground()` in their :new — CONFIRMED.**
- ISPanel.lua:9-11 — `function ISPanel:noBackground() self.background = false; end`
- ISPanel.lua:17-22 — prerender draws bg+border ONLY when `self.background` is truthy
- All 5 vanilla call sites (ISCharacterScreen, ISCharacterInfo, ISCharacterProtection, ISHealthPanel, ISClothingInsPanel) cited in plan; vanilla universal pattern.

**Claim 4: Construction-time wrap captures stale dims — CONFIRMED.**
- Our wrap captures `panelW = self.charScreen and self.charScreen.width or self.width` AFTER origCreateChildren returns. ISCharacterInfoWindow.lua:152-153 has just set self.width = self.charScreen.width from constructor-time charScreen dims. ISCharacterScreen first render hasn't fired yet to grow itself. Captured panelW is stale on first render and never updated.

**Locked design choice for Tasks 2-3:** Issue A → `o:noBackground()` (canonical vanilla helper). Issue B → BOTH override-setWidth/setHeight AND per-frame parent-dim sample (NOT redundant — per-frame sample is PRIMARY working mechanism since vanilla doesn't propagate; override is defensive secondary that fires when sample calls self:setWidth, routing children through reflowLayout). Per-frame sample reads `self.parent:getWidth()` and `self.parent:getHeight() - self.parent.tabHeight`.
