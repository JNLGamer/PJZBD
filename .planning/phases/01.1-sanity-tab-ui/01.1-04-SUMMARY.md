# Plan 01.1-04 Summary — GAP-01 / GAP-02 Closure

**Status:** Closed (gaps resolved; new gaps discovered during UAT — see Plan 05)

## What was built

Plan 04 surgically rewrote `SanityPanel:drawSanityBar` to render a vertical right-edge sanity bar with an always-on green→yellow→red gradient backdrop, replacing the original horizontal top-of-panel bar with single-color sanity-tinted fill. The render layout in `SanityPanel:render` was reflowed: numeric readout and stage label moved from below-the-bar (vanilla horizontal layout) to top-left of the panel; event log width was reduced by 32px to reserve a right-edge bar column. UI-SPEC.md was amended with the new vertical-bar contract, always-on gradient rule, and explicit provenance notes mapping each amendment to GAP-01 / GAP-02 in VERIFICATION.md.

## Why this plan

The original Plan 03 implementation followed CONTEXT.md decision D-05 (3-stop gradient interpretation) and D-21 (top-to-bottom horizontal layout), which produced a horizontal bar across the top of the panel that changed color with sanity (green at full, transitioning toward red as sanity dropped). User in-game UAT (Phase 01.1 first round) reported two design preferences:
- **GAP-01**: bar should be vertical pinned to the right side, not horizontal across the top
- **GAP-02**: gradient should ALWAYS show the full green→yellow→red rainbow (sanity controls fill HEIGHT, not fill COLOR — like a thermometer or progress bar showing "you've used X% of your full range")

CONTEXT.md decisions are LOCKED HISTORY (history-of-decisions integrity); the visual contract was instead amended in UI-SPEC.md with provenance to the gap entries.
